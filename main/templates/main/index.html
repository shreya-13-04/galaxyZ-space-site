About GalaxyZ Space

Inspiring the next generation of space scientists and explorers

Our Mission

We believe in making space science accessible to everyone. Our mission is to inspire curiosity and passion for the cosmos through interactive education.

Expert Educators

Our workshops are led by professionals with backgrounds in astronomy, astrophysics, and aerospace engineering, bringing real-world expertise to your learning journey.

Innovative Approach

Using cutting-edge technology and immersive experiences, we make complex space concepts engaging and understandable for learners of all ages.
